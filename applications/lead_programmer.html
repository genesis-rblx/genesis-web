Genesis Space

Genesis Space was founded under the belief that there should be a future where all vehicles are reusable and capable of going beyond our home planet, without incurring a huge cost price in a cost-effective manner. In order to achieve this, we are in a constant need of fresh minds and ideas, supplied by people who look to contribute to this goal. These people, in order to communicate their ideas with us, require a safe and reliable interface for communicating with other members within the corporation. Programmers are the creators and maintainers of this interface, who work hard to ensure that operations run smoothly outside of ROBLOX, and avert any obstacles that newcomers may face.

Lead Programmer

The programmer creates and maintains systems which include, but not limited to, the main website, communication interfaces, webhooks, web API's, interprocess telemetry broadcasting and more. In comparison to ROBLOX scripters, programmers are expected to be knowledgable about the specific field they specialize in (web development, graphics, cryptography), and adhere to programming patterns in order to facilitate collaboration and create scalable programs. Programmers may be given tasks that are not related to their specialization, should there be a deficiency of required programmers, but are however, still expected to find an appropriate solution.

As a programmer, you are expected to be up-to-date with the latest changes in the computer industry, and maintain systems such that it adheres to modern standards. You will be also expected to work in a team and collaborate frequently to find efficient solutions.

One of the main priorities at Genesis, is to ensure that our staff are in a good condition and provide a suitable environment to foster productivity in individuals. To ensure this, you are expected to be honest about any problems that you may face as an employee of Genesis. The Programming Department works with the HR department to report any misconduct and create a healthy work environment with routinely enforced work ethics.

Should you face any problem as a newcomer within in the company, members in the Programming Department are readily available to support you and resolve your queries.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain non-ROBLOX systems to ensure smooth operation and enhanced productivity of the users
  • Present and express your ideas and your vision for the future of the company outside ROBLOX
  • Participate and collaborate with other individuals within the department
  • Solve problems or participate in debugging by logically reasoning with the support of your teammates, and conclude with proper, efficient solutions

Basic Qualifications

  • You must specialize in at least one language, and have basic knowledge in two (We currently require developers who specialize in Python, C# with .NET Framework, or JavaScript)
  • 1 year of playing the platform Roblox.
  • You must be above 14 years old.
  • You must have basic knowledge of the Git VCS
  • You must have a account on GitHub

Additional Requirements

  • You may be asked to work for an extended time period depending on different launch and testing cadences.

Incentives to work at Genesis Space

In the future, we will be paying all Programmers within Genesis Space, the payment will be based on your tasks and how fast you finish them.

As of right now, applying for this job allows you to have a lot of positive benefits, a key benefit is that you can meet new and smart people in the team with who you can work together flawlessly.

As a Programmer, you are eligible for long-term Robux dividends, meaning you can receive a long-term Robux award up to 2k Robux depending on how well you do throughout half a year period.
